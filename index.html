Welcome to my website, I'm Rachel

UX Designer, Book Lover, Fashion Enthusiast, Coffee Addict, Organization Junky

Hello, my name is Rachel Bradley and I’m so glad you’ve decided to stop by my website. I’m a UX designer from Arizona with a background studying economics. I love human behavior and have a creative side, so a career in UX was inevitable for me.

I care about making a website that is easy for users to navigate and gets rid of the headache we all so often feel browsing websites and apps. I want to make the lives of users easier while sticking to the branding of a company and making the user fall in love with the aesthetics they see.
