Convincing You To Read

A personal book blog that includes ratings and reviews of all the books I've read recently and lists of the ones I want to read.

PROJECT OVERVIEW

My Role

Lead UX/UI Designer

Deliverables

High-Fidelity Mobile and Desktop Prototypes

Tools I Used

AdobeXD, Github, Visual Studio Code, Miro, Google Slides, Excel, Word

Timeline

04/10/22-04/17/22 User Research, Definition and Ideation

04/18/22-05/18/22 Prototyping, Testing, Iterations

05/16/22-05/25/22 Final Iterations

I came up with the idea for creating my own personalized book blog because of my own love for books and experiencing a lack of options for online book reviews. I noticed fellow book club members and friends experiencing the same issues as me and wanted to provide a blog that makes finding reviews of popular books easy and fun.

USER RESEARCH

Proto Persona

The first step in my research was to come up with a user I believe would want to visit and stay on the website.

Heuristic Evaluation

I then conducted a heuristic evaluation of three popular book review websites. Goodreads, Library Thing, and Reedsy Discovery.

User Research Plan and Interviews

Research Question:Why would users want a book review blog?

Objective 1: As a user researcher, I want to understand users frustrations with current book review sites.

Objective 2: As a user research, I want to understand why users aren't using book review websites.

Objective 3: As a user researcher, I want to understand what would get a user excited to visit a book review website.

Affinity Diagram

An affinity diagram was put together using the results from the in person interview.

Empathy Map

An empathy map was then put together to further understand the users.

IDEATION

Problem Statment

We believe creating a user friendly book blog with detailed trusted reviews will lead our users to gain a trust for the website, recommend the site to their friends, and visit again in the future.

User Scenario

I then put together a user scenario to show how our user persona would feel towards the blog.

Value Proposition Statement

My book blog is creating content for book lovers to find honest and quality book recommendation of all genres. We're better because the website is frequently updated, easier to navigate than other popular book sites, and is aesthetically pleasing to look at. The site is believable becasue there is no affiliating with authors or businesses trying to sell certain books.

LOW-FI Wireframes

Low-Fi Prototype

The first step to putting all the research together was creating low-fit wireframes.

USER TESTING AND OUTCOMES

User Tests

I learned from user testing and feedback that some additional iterations needed to be made. Such as adding a star rating to the reviews page, being able to filter by rating, and changing the reading lists.
