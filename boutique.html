Increasing Confidence Using Fashion

Creation of a clothing boutique website for women ready to transition to a more sophisticated style. This website is designed to create a calm, cozy, and inviting atmosphere inspiring its users to take their style to the next level.

USER RESEARCH

User Research Plan and Interviews

As a researcher, I began my study by conducting user interviews. My primary focus was on understanding the perspectives of women aged 23 to 32 regarding their shopping experiences, style preferences, and how it influenced their self-confidence. Their answers would be important in deciding how to move forward with the direction of the website.

Research Question: What makes a clothing website stand out?

Objective 1: As a user researcher, I want to understand what makes users want to visit a clothing website.

Objective 2: As a user research, I want to understand what makes users want to purchase from a clothing website.

Objective 3: As a user researcher, I want to understand how their clothing purchases make them feel post shopping.

Proto-Persona

After conducting the user interviews, the next step in my research plan was to create a proto-persona. Drawing inspiration from the insights gained during the interviews.

Competitor Analysis

To gain a comprehensive understanding of the pain points experienced by users I conducted a competitor analysis of three prominent companies in the fashion industry: Pull and Bear, H&M, and Zara. I selected these competitors based on mentions of them from participants during the user interviews.

IDEATION

Value Proposition Statement

Vêtements is an upscale online clothing boutique that offers stylish and sophisticated fashion for women aged 23-32 who are transitioning from youthful to more refined styles. This boutique has trendy and high-quality clothing, making it the perfect destination for women who desire to exude confidence and style. Vêtements' mission is to empower women to express themselves through fashion, whether they're out on the town or crushing it in the workplace.

Storyboard

To create a more immersive experience and visually depict the user journey, a storyboard was developed. This storyboard was based on insights gathered from user interviews and the creation of the proto-persona.

LOW-FI PROTOTYPING

Low-Fi Prototype

At the beginning of the design process, I created a basic low-fidelity prototype to observe how users interacted with the layout. The prototype aimed to simplify the navigation and create a less overwhelming user experience compared to other fashion websites.

USER TESTING AND OUTCOMES

User Tests

I conducted three user tests to see how users interacted with the website. Through my observations and additional feedback I got some valuable insight into how users were feeling about and interacting with the low-fidelity website. I decided to make serveal iterations:

1. Put cart all the way to the right of the page.

2. Add a number to the cart to indicate how many items have been added.

3. Have less text on the website. It was feeling overwhelming to users.

4. Add best sellers and new arrivals.

5. Be able to click on "shop" and not just the dropdown menu.

6. Get rid of FAQ, Users didn't interact or care about it.

7. Be able to revisit items from cart to determine if the user actually wants the item.

STYLE TILE AND HIGH-FI PROTOTYPING

Style Guide

Given that the primary goal of the website is to assist women in transitioning to a more sophisticated and chic style, it was important to ensure that the website embodied those qualities as well. To achieve this, I focused on creating a design that was simple, modern, and feminine, while also providing a seamless and engaging user experience.
