Increasing Confidence Using Fashion

Creation of a clothing boutique website designed to make it easier for women to transition to a more sophisticated style, resulting in more confidence and professionalism for the users and increased profits and returning customers for the clothing brand.

PROJECT OVERVIEW

My Role

Lead UX/UI Designer

Deliverables

High-Fidelity Mobile and Desktop Prototypes

Tools I Used

AdobeXD, Github, Visual Studio Code, Miro, Google Slides, Excel, Word

USER RESEARCH

User Research Plan and Interviews

As a researcher, I began my study by conducting 5 user interviews. My primary focus was on understanding the perspectives of women aged 24 to 38 regarding their shopping experiences, style preferences, if they struggled to find age appropriate clothing, and how it influenced their self-confidence. Their answers would be important in deciding how to move forward with the focus of the website.

Research Results

80% of the women interviewed admitted to having their confidence decrease after shopping online or receiving clothing purchases from online shopping.

60% expressed difficulty in finding clothing that fit their style. One interviewer said:

"it feels like everything looks like something you would wear while going out to the bar with your friends in college, but I'm not even in college anymore!"

40% said they view shopping as a core.

Proto-Persona

After conducting the user interviews, the next step in my research plan was to create a proto-persona. Drawing inspiration from the insights gained during the interviews. This would assist me in seeing my users more clearly and having a point of reference while making the website.

Competitor Analysis

To gain a more comprehensive understanding of the pain points experienced by users while shopping online I conducted competitor analyses of three prominent companies in the fashion industry: Pull and Bear, H&M, and Zara. I selected these competitors based on mentions of them from participants during the user interviews and their high level of influence on the current fashion market.

IDEATION

Value Proposition Statement

Vêtements is an upscale online clothing boutique that offers stylish and sophisticated fashion for women aged 24-38 who are transitioning from youthful to more refined styles. This boutique has trendy and high-quality clothing, making it the perfect destination for women who desire to exude confidence and style. Vêtements' mission is to empower women to express themselves through fashion, whether they're out on the town or crushing it in the workplace.

Storyboard

To create a more immersive experience and visually depict the user journey, a storyboard was developed. This storyboard was based on insights gathered from user interviews and the creation of the proto-persona.

LOW-FI PROTOTYPING

Low-Fi Prototype

At the beginning of the design process, I created a low-fidelity prototype to observe how users interacted with the layout. The prototype aimed to simplify the complicated navigation often found on clothing websites and create a less overwhelming user experience.

USER TESTING AND OUTCOMES

User Tests

I conducted three user tests to see how users interacted with the website. Through my observations and additional feedback I got some valuable insight into how users were feeling about and interacting with the low-fidelity website. I decided to make serveal iterations:

1. Put cart all the way to the right of the page.

2. Add a number to the cart to indicate how many items have been added.

3. Have less text on the website. It was feeling overwhelming to users.

4. Add best sellers and new arrivals.

5. Be able to click on "shop" and not just the dropdown menu.

6. Get rid of FAQ, Users didn't interact or care about it.

7. Be able to revisit items from cart to determine if the user actually wants the item.

STYLE TILE AND HIGH-FI PROTOTYPING

Style Guide

Given that the primary goal of the website is to assist women in transitioning to a more sophisticated and chic style, it was important to ensure that the website embodied those qualities as well. To achieve this, I focused on creating a design that was simple, modern, and feminine, while also providing a seamless and engaging user experience.
